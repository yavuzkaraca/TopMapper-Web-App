About TopMapper

TopMapper is a web-based simulation tool designed to model topologic projections and axon guidance mechanisms during neural development. Evolving from its original Python-based backend, Top Mapper now offers an interactive and accessible interface for researchers to simulate and visualize complex biological processes.

Getting Started

To explore TopMapper, head to the Simulation tab. Here, you can set up the parameters for your simulation and launch it. After the simulation completes, the results will be displayed. If you’re interested in modifying the simulation logic, you’ll find an open-source link available. For foundational knowledge on the model, visit the Papers tab for relevant resources.
